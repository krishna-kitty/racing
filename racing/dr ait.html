WANT TO KNOW MORE

Skill Development: Participating in a student formula event provides a platform for students to Develop technical skill such as design,fabrication, and skill such as communication,teamwork, and project management

Exposure: Formula events often attract a large audience, including industry professionals and potential employers, giving students the opportunity to showcase their work and network with industry leaders.

Challenge: Formula events provide a unique challenge that cannot be replicated in a classroom setting. It requires students to apply their theoretical knowledge in a practical and competitive environment, which helps to build confidence and resilience.

Innovation: The formula event requires students to think innovatively and creatively to overcome design and engineering challenges. This experience can be invaluable to their future careers.

Reputation: Participating in a formula event can enhance the reputation of the institution and the sponsor supporting the team, as it demonstrates a commitment to fostering innovation and education among students.

Known more

Lightweight materials: We have utilized lightweight materials such as carbon fiber and aluminum to reduce the overall weight of the car, which improves the acceleration and handling performance.

Aerodynamic design: Our car has been designed with advanced aerodynamic features, such as a front and rear wing, diffusers, and air dams, which minimize drag and increase downforce to improve stability and cornering performance.

Electric powertrain: We have implemented an electric powertrain, which is more efficient, environmentally friendly, and produces instant torque for better acceleration and performance.

Advanced data acquisition system: Our car is equipped with an advanced data acquisition system, which collects and analyzes real-time data such as speed, temperature, and battery status to optimize the car's performance and ensure its reliability.

Safety features: We have incorporated various safety features such as a roll cage, fire suppression system, and impact-absorbing materials to ensure the safety of the driver and passengers.
